About

I am a Software Engineer and Consultant with 9+ years of experience designing and managing scalable AI, data and infrastructure systems across research and industry domains.

My work spans end-to-end system ownership — from developing AI-driven applications and data platforms to managing GPU clusters, distributed databases, secure servers, backups and production web systems.
